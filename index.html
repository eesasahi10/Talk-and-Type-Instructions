Talk&Type

How to use the Talk&Type App

Step 1: Download the Talk&Type App
Go to your app store or play store to download the Talk&Type app
Step 2: Enter the code
Open the app, then open talk-and-type.onrender.com on your laptop. After that, enter the 7-digit code you see on the laptop. Also make sure you download the desktop app as well.
Step 3: Start speaking
Once you are done typing in the code and also downloading the app, then choose your language and start speaking. You will see whatever you speak, it will be typed onto the computer.

Frequentry Asked Questions

❓ How to fix "Unknown Publisher" (Windows)

1. When the blue box appears, look for the small text that says "More Info".

2. Click it, and a new button will appear: "Run Anyway".

3. Click that, and Talk&Type will start immediately!

Can I not download the desktop app?

No! You must download the desktop app for the app to work.

How to use the desktop app

To use the desktop app, you have to click on the button, and ignore the warnings as the publisher is independant. Once downloaded, open the app and now, you can ignore the black screen and the computer will start typing anywhere you want
